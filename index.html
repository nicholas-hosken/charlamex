Marisol Plata Fortiz

Spanish, English and French Teacher & Translator

Mobile: +52 1 998 243 6678


Originally from Tlaxcala, in the heart of Mexico, Marisol was first recognised for her skill in languages by Carlos Slim who offered her a bursary and paid, in full, for all her studies at "Universidad Aut√≥noma de Tlaxcala". Upon obtaining her BA in Modern Languages she was imediatly hired by the "Thomas More College" in Chelsea, London. Here she assisted students from as young as 12 to succeed in developing conversational Spanish.

While living in London she continued her studies first at "The Burlington School of English" and later at "The London Metropolitan University" where she finally obtained a Masters in Applied Translation. Continuing her studies at St George International, she also completed TESOL (Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages).

To subsidise her studying, she started offering private lessons in the evenings which turned out to be so popular that she eventually started teaching privately full time. London, being the melting pot that it is, allowed her teach students of all levels and from various countries including: Belarus, Bolivia, Brazil, Canada, Colombia, France, Germany, Hungary, Italy, Japan, Poland, South Africa, Venezuela and the UK.

"Everyone has the potential to succeed and I get a real sense of achievement helping students to achieve their goals. However, I am a traditional tutor in that I expect the student to also work hard, fully commit, complete set work and prepare properly for lessons. Good 'Preparation' is everything. I can give students the tools and show them how to use them but it is essentially up to them to do the work."
